About Us

Premium laundry care, tailored for Vizag.

Welcome to Battalu, the leading on-demand laundry and dry-cleaning service dedicated to serving the vibrant city of Vizag. We understand that your time is valuable, and spending it sorting, washing, and ironing clothes shouldn't get in the way of what you love doing.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide a seamless, premium, and reliable laundry experience right at your doorstep. We combine modern technology with expert fabric care to ensure your clothes are treated with the utmost respect, returned to you fresh, crisp, and perfectly folded or pressed.

Why We Started

Battalu was born out of a desire to modernize the traditional laundry experience. We saw the need for a transparent, efficient service that didn't just clean clothes, but cared for them. From eco-friendly cleaning solvents to our state-of-the-art washing facilities, every step of our process is designed to extend the life of your garments.

Our Commitment

We are committed to transparent pricing with absolutely no hidden fees, real-time order tracking so you always know where your clothes are, and exceptional customer service. When you choose Battalu, you're choosing a partner dedicated to making your life easier.
